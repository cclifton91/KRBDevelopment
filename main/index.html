About Us

We are hear to bring equitable stability to blighted under served communities.

My name is Ken Brown, I am a real estate developer with a passion for helping those in under privileged communities. I grew up on the south side of Chicago and saw the lack of resources for good housing, as well as the negative impact of red lining. It made me start thinking about affordable housing for people who want better neighborhoods and communities no matter what the economic or social background. I have 30 years of experience in the real estate and financing industry, and my goal is use that experience to help those who have trouble getting funding for their business. KRB Development is here to make sure that equity and equitable stability is a part of every community.
